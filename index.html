TaraGO is a ride-sharing service that offers convenient and affordable land transportation options. We connect passengers with drivers through a mobile app, allowing users to request rides, track their driver's location, and pay for their trips digitally. TaraGo aims to provide a safe, reliable, and hassle-free alternative to traditional taxi services.

How it works

The process begins with Step 1: Dashboard Overview, where the user lands on the main screen displaying their profile details and an overview of today's ride. Key features on this screen include a search bar for navigation or filtering, a ride summary showing route details, estimated time of arrival, and the time remaining for the driver's arrival.

Moving to Step 2: Enter Destination, the user selects or enters their destination on the "Enter Your Destination" screen. They can choose their current location ("My Location"), specific addresses or streets ( "Folsom St."), or a list of recent destinations for quicker selection. Finally, in Step 3: Booking Confirmation, after confirming the destination, the user proceeds to book their ride. A success screen displays the message: "You have successfully booked a Ride," accompanied by a friendly note at the bottom: "Have a nice Travel."
